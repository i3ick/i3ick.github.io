Graduation short about a young Polish partisan fleeing from the Warsaw Uprising.

DOGS

Synopsis:


A young Polish partisan flees from the Warsaw Uprising. Whilst hiding in the yard of a countryside manor, he is chased up a tree by a large wolfhound. With his rifle out of reach, there seems to be no way to escape his predicament.

About:


Dogs was our graduation movie at Gobelins, Paris. We all directed and animated the short together. My main contribution was in the layout, animation, and compositing phases.
